About Me

This is not what I do.

Definitely not a Web Developer by nature. My passion has always been with data beginning first as a Database Administrator. Through the years I was involved in numerous projects that involved more Database Development than Administration and always found those projects more exciting. Spent some time as a Senior Business Intelligence Developer before landing in my current role as Enterprise Data Architect. That role has lead me away from your standard on-prem architectures to Cloud-based architectures, which leads me to this web-site. While most of my time is spent designing and creating data pipelines, I felt it was in my best interest to learn about the actual environment I will be building my designs on. In my case, AWS. So here I am, using a small sampling of AWS offerings to build a website based on Python (Flask). I want to be clear that while I am embarking on this endeavor in an effort to further my professional career, this web-site is entirely personal and in no way represents my employer's views or opinions.
